Custom hand painted tiles add long lasting beauty and can transform your living space into an extraordinary element of daily life. From durable maintenance free signage to attention grabbing entry ways your piece from Fine Art Tile is a permanent expression of who you are. From bowls of luscious fruits and vegetables, to fields of flowers, or an underwater wonderland, the possibilities are endless. Each image is designed and painted by hand on bisque, porcelain, terra cotta, or your own glazed field tiles. Liquid glazes are mixed and combined to blend your chosen palette with the surrounding elements. Tiles are fired at 1800 degrees enabling these pieces to be installed indoors or out. Hand painted tiles are able to withstand years of Rocky Mountain winters and blazing tropical summers. By providing us with pictures, fabrics, colors, and themes, we can coordinate your dreams with your budget, making each hand painted tile project a complete success.
